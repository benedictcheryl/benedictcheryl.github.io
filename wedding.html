Our Story

We originally wanted to use this space to share a bit more about us and our story. However, looking back at our relationship for the past 6+ years, we realise that there is simply no way to encapsulate the journey we've had within a few simple paragraphs. But if you're reading this, you've probably been part of our lives in one way or another, and make up the bits and pieces of our story. So to all of you – those who've guided, encouraged, edified, and loved us in your own unique ways – a big thank you. It is our great delight and honour to invite you to witness our wedding and share in our joy and celebration!
