Our Story

19, wide-eyed, naïve in love
Holding hands we leapt in faith
To love and to be loved by you
A glimpse of love much greater

But our sinful flesh betrays us
Selfish love's not love at all
For can two broken lives expect
Unbrokenness together?

So at the cross on bended knee
Our lives in full surrender
I cannot give you perfect love
Only point to One who is

To love you as Christ loves the church
We’ll run this race together
We don’t know what the future holds
But do know He who holds it
